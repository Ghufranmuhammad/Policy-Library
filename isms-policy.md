Information Security Management System (ISMS) Policy

Purpose

To establish a framework for protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information at DesertTech Solutions LLC.

---

Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, third parties, systems, and information assets within the ISMS scope.

---

Objectives

- Protect information assets
- Reduce cybersecurity risks
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
- Support continual improvement of the ISMS

---

Management Commitment

Top management is committed to providing resources, supporting security initiatives, approving policies, and promoting a culture of information security.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Management provides leadership and resources.

The GRC team manages compliance and risk activities.

The IT team implements technical controls.

Employees follow policies and report security incidents.

---

Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or whenever significant business or security changes occur.
